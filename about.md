    <h1>About Us</h1>

    <p class="lead">The Charleston Amateur Radio Society (CARS) was established in 1971 and is an ARRL affiliated club with approximately 170 members.</p>

    <h2>Our Mission</h2>
    <p>CARS supports the communication needs of numerous events and organizations throughout the South Carolina Lowcountry. We are dedicated to advancing amateur radio through education, public service, and fellowship.</p>

    <h2>Meeting Information</h2>
    <p>CARS meets on the <strong>second Monday of every month at 7pm</strong> at the Golden Corral near Tanger Outlets in North Charleston. Visitors are always welcome - you don't need to purchase a meal to attend!</p>

    <h2>Facilities & Operations</h2>
    <p>The club maintains a room aboard the USS Yorktown at Patriots Point, alongside an emergency trailer. Both spaces feature 2m/70cm FM, UHF DMR, and HF capabilities.</p>
    <p>CARS sponsors or maintains many of the 2m and 70cm repeaters in the Lowcountry and operates three APRS digipeaters.</p>

    <h2>Community Involvement</h2>
    <p>We support major events including:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>ARRL Field Day</li>
      <li>Museum Ships Weekend</li>
      <li>Cooper River Bridge Run & Walk</li>
      <li>Hurricane preparedness expos</li>
    </ul>
    <p>The club also sponsors the DuBose Middle School Amateur Radio Club (K4DMS) as an ARRL educational initiative since 2004.</p>

    <h2>Newsletter</h2>
    <p>Members receive the <strong>CARS Carrier</strong>, our monthly newsletter with club news, technical articles, and upcoming events.</p>
