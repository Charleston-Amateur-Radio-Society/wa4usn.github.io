We are pleased to announce a new Memorandum of Understanding with Patriots Point Naval & Maritime Museum for Museum Ships Weekend operations.

This agreement formalizes our long-standing relationship with Patriots Point and ensures continued access to the USS Yorktown CV-10 for special event amateur radio operations.

## What This Means

- Guaranteed access for Museum Ships Weekend
- Opportunity for additional special event operations
- Continued partnership for educational outreach
- Support for maritime mobile operations

We thank the Patriots Point staff for their continued support of amateur radio and our club's activities aboard the historic USS Yorktown.
